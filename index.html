Welcome to Rekai

A homebrew world for Dungeons and Dragons, 5th Edition

The Current Climate

Temples report their holy light has grown weaker, the sun seems to be struggling to rise, graves are being robbed of their corpses, a red symbol is sometimes seen in the sky, masses of vermin are swarming larger cities, mages are finding it harder to concentrate on their spells, women are giving birth too rapidly for their bodies to keep up, winds blow hot and storms seem never ending...and amidst it all war threatens the horizon.
